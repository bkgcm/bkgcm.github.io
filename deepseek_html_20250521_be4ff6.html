About NUMINA

NUMINA is the official e-magazine of the Department of Mathematics at Bijoy Krishna Girls' College, Howrah. Established in 2020, our mission is to foster a deeper appreciation for mathematics among students and the wider community.

The name "NUMINA" is derived from the Latin word meaning "divine will" or "spirit," reflecting our belief in the transcendent beauty and power of mathematical concepts.

Our Mission

To create an engaging platform that showcases the beauty, utility, and excitement of mathematics through student and faculty contributions, while developing communication and research skills among our students.

Our Vision

To become a leading student mathematics publication that inspires young women to pursue mathematical studies and careers, while serving as a model for other collegiate mathematics departments.
